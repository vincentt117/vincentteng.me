DCarbon

A Google Chrome Extension activates while the user is on Google Maps. It calculates the travel distance and multiplies that by tailpipe emission grams of CO2/mi of the registered vehicle's found on the EPA database. We take that number and using the formula provided by the Arbor day foundation (15 dollar offsets 1 million grams of C02 emission) gives the user the opportunity to donate to the Arbor day foundation (which plants a tree for every dollar donated)

Finalist for Best Environment & Sustainability Hack @ HackTech hosted at CalTech

Spicy AI

A web-service built on Node incorporating Nuance's MIX API, IBM's Watson Tone Analysis API, and embodiment of giphy searches. The services takes a spoken phrase and converts it to string via MIX API, sends it to Tone Analysis for evaluation of emotional value (a score for each of five emotions: joy, fear, anger, disgust, and sadness), the emotion of greatest magnitude is then used to search through giphy, where relevant .GIF loops are presented to the user on the web-app.
